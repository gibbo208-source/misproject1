About Me

Thank you for visiting Carson Gibbons' personal website. Here you will find information about me and my plans for the future!

My name is Carson Gibbons, a student studying MIS at UMD's Labovitz School of Business and Economics. I have a wide range of interests and ambitions. Hopefully this website will help any visitors get to know me better.

My two favorite hobbies are collecting video games and reading. I have done both since childhood and both have matured along with me. Both have given me an immense amount of joy throughout my life thus far and will continue to in the future.

I am a lifelong resident of Northern Minnesota, currently studying MIS at the University of Minnesota Duluth's Labovitz School of Business and Economics. My interests include video games, history, philosophy, and 19th-century literature. I also enjoy writing essays and fiction.

I have held the same job for over five years as a superintendent and maintenance man at a large apartment complex. My skills include plumbing, basic electrical work, and landscaping.
