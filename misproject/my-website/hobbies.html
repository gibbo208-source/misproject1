My Hobbies

Discover the passions that drive my interests beyond academics and work.

Video Game Collection

Since adolescence, I have acquired a large video game collection. I prefer to buy games physically. My favorite generation to collect for is the sixth generation (i.e., PS2, Xbox, GameCube, Dreamcast). I also have a sizable console collection, starting with the second generation of consoles. Additionally, I have a large digital PC library. In some ways, I like buying video games more than playing them.

Writing

I have always enjoyed writing; it is the most effective form of communication, in my opinion, as it allows one to curate their thoughts and ideas before sending them to others. The beauty of language can also be showcased far more effectively through the written word.

Other Interests

Beyond gaming and writing, I have a deep appreciation for history, philosophy, and 19th-century literature. These subjects provide endless opportunities for intellectual exploration and help shape my worldview.
